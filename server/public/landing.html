Legal & policies

Our policies

Last updated: June 2026 · Questions? hello@metalix.in

TL;DR: Cancel any time before "Printing started" for a full refund. If we make a mistake, we reprint free. If the issue is with your file, we can offer a paid reprint.

When can I cancel?

You can cancel your order free of charge from My Orders on the app as long as the status is Received or Queued. The moment the status changes to Printing Started, your job is on the press and can no longer be cancelled.

If the mistake is ours

We take responsibility for errors that happen on our end — smudged pages, wrong paper size, wrong number of copies, wrong color mode applied after you paid for auto-detect. In these cases:

  • We'll reprint the affected pages free of charge, or
  • Issue a full or partial refund to your original payment method within 5–7 business days

Report the issue within 24 hours of pickup or delivery by calling or WhatsApp-ing us.

If the issue is with your file

We print exactly what you upload, without modifying your files. We cannot offer refunds for:

  • Low-resolution images that appear blurry once printed
  • Typos or content errors in the original document
  • Wrong file uploaded by mistake
  • Color pages the customer chose to print as B/W (or vice versa)

We're happy to offer a paid reprint once you've updated your file.

Refund timeline

Approved refunds are processed to your original payment method. Razorpay typically settles refunds within 5–7 business days.

TL;DR: Pickup is free, home delivery is ₹30 flat. Most orders are ready within 3–4 hours of payment.

Delivery options

We offer two ways to receive your prints:

  • Shop pickup — Free. Collect from our store at your convenience once we notify you.
  • Home delivery — ₹30 flat fee within the city. We dispatch your order as soon as it's printed.

Turnaround time

Most standard orders (under 100 pages) are ready within 3–4 hours of successful payment confirmation. Bulk orders (100+ pages or multiple copies) may take longer — we'll estimate the time at checkout.

Delivery area

Home delivery is currently available within Gurugram city limits. If your PIN code is outside our zone, we'll contact you to arrange shop pickup and refund the delivery charge.

Delays

If your order is delayed significantly past the estimate, reach out via WhatsApp with your Order ID. We'll either expedite or waive the delivery fee, depending on the situation.

Who can use this service

Metalix Print is available to anyone aged 18 and above. By placing an order, you agree to these terms.

Your content

You confirm that you have the legal right to print and reproduce the content in any file you upload. Metalix Print is not responsible for copyright infringement, defamatory content, or unauthorized reproduction of third-party material.

What we will not print

  • Content that infringes copyright without authorization
  • Defamatory, obscene, or hateful material
  • Content that violates any applicable law

Payment

All payments are processed securely through Razorpay. Metalix Print does not store your card or banking details. By paying, you agree to Razorpay's terms of service.

Limitation of liability

Our liability is limited to the amount paid for the specific order in question. We are not liable for indirect losses, loss of business, or consequential damages.

Short version: Your files are used only to print your order and deleted after 3 days. We don't sell your data.

What we collect

  • Name, mobile number, and optional email — to process and communicate about your order
  • Delivery address — only if you choose home delivery
  • Uploaded files — solely to fulfil your print job
  • Payment transaction data — processed by Razorpay; we only receive a transaction ID

How we use it

We use your information only to fulfil your order, contact you about it, and improve our service. We do not share your data with third parties except as needed to complete delivery (our delivery partner).

File retention

Uploaded documents are automatically and permanently deleted from our systems 3 days after your order is completed.

Your rights

You can request deletion of your personal data or a copy of what we hold by emailing hello@metalix.in.
